About Krishna

Made in the forests of southern India.

I create dreamlike images.

It started with The Jungle Book. I was mesmerised by the woods, and convinced that I’d get hypnotised by Kaa. Eventually, I bought a camera, led the photography club at college, and somewhere between then and now the forests of southern India became the centre of my life.

Here the herds move like weather and the cats let you see them only when they choose. There is no better feeling than watching the wilderness simply be itself.

Very grateful to the people who walked this path with me, helped me along it, and inspired me with their own work. The wild has taught me more than anything else I can name. It has shaped the person I am.

The Mystical Wild is what I bring back: chapters of a forest that owes nobody anything. Every frame was made in the wild, spending hours tracking each animal.

If a frame stayed with you, write to me. I won’t bite :)
